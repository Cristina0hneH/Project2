Paneer Pizza Recipe

03, August 2022
Total Making Time: 20 mins

Pizza Size:

How to make Paneer Pizza:

Step 1 Spread layers of butter and pizza sauce Take a ready-made pizza crust or a base. If you love healthy eating, then go for wheat or multi-grain base. Spread a layer of butter on the pizza base, followed by pizza sauce.

Step 2 Place cottage cheese cubes along with capsicum If you do not have pizza sauce at home, you can use tomato ketchup. Chop the paneer into cubes and slice the capsicum. Once done, add a layer of grated cheese (keep the half to use as a topping) to the base and place paneer cubes and capsicum slices on the pizza base.

Step 3 Bake the pizza Now, spread the remaining cheese on top of the veggies. Ensure that it spreads evenly all over the pizza. Ideally, you must use mozzarella cheese, but if you do not have it, you can use cheese cubes as well. Bake the pizza at 180 degrees C for 10-15 minutes, depending on your oven.

Step 4 Cool and cut the pizza into slices, serve hot Once done, remove the pizza and allow it to cool slightly. Cut the paneer pizza into slices. Garnish the pizza by sprinkling oregano seasoning or chilli flakes on top and Serve hot. Do try this delicious recipe, rate it and leave your comments in the section below.

Index Measurements Ingredients
1 1 medium Medium Pizza Base
2 100 gm Cubed Paneer
3 1/2 cup Mozzarella
4 1 tablespoon pizza sauce
5 1 cubed Capsicum
